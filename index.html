Gender in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy


Welcome to the homepage of the SEP Gender Project. The purpose of this project is twofold:

1. To present data on authorship and citation patterns regarding women philosophers in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy, and to draw attention to how rates of authorship and citation vary between areas of philosophy.

2. To highlight women philosophers by providing a database of their works cited in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy.
