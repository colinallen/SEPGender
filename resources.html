Additional Resources


APA Resources on Diversity and Inclusiveness

The American Philosophical Association has compiled an extensive list of resources on diversity and inclusiveness. Find inclusive syllabi, articles and reading lists, best practices information, projects, programs, and data.


Hypatia

Hypatia is a journal of feminist philosophy committed to promote the diversity within feminism, feminist philosophy, and the broader field of philosophy.


Women in Philosophy: Quantitative Analysis of Specialization, Prevalence, Visibility, and Generational Change

This study by Eric Shwitzgebel & Carolyn Dicey Jennings includes several quantitative analyses of the prevalence of women in political, social, and moral philosophy. Findings include that Ethics is the field of philosophy closest to gender parity and that women's involvement in philosophy has increased since the 1970s.
